Ideal Jobs:

Although all group members want to pursue different jobs in IT, all group members have to possess a general collection of skills.

Some of these skills are:

  • Continue to stay up to date about current practices and be able to adapt.
  • Possess strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Manage projects to agreed deadlines.
  • Have strong communication skills
  • Experience with AWS

The main aspect that sets each group member apart is the specialisation of each job. Tim specialises in back-end development for health systems at Apple Inc.
