Ideal Jobs:

Although all group members want to pursue different jobs in IT, all group members have to possess a general collection of skills.

Some of these skills are:

  • Continue to stay up to date about current practices and be able to adapt.
  • Possess strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Manage projects to agreed deadlines.
  • Have strong communication skills
  • Experience with AWS

The main aspect that sets each group member apart is the specialisation of each job. Tim specialises in back-end development for health systems at Apple Inc. Vanessa specialises in data analysis projects that help determine business outcomes. Josh specialises in creating cloud, data and digital software solutions. Finally, Mitchell specialises in program and project management for a data analytics organisation.
